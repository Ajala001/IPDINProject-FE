Who We Are

The Institute of Professional Driving Instructors of Nigeria (IPDIN) is a national body created to bridge the gap in formal driving instructor education. Our mission is to reduce road accidents by raising the quality of instruction and standardizing training.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To professionalize driving instruction in Nigeria through certified training that produces safer, more responsible drivers.

Mission: Raise safety awareness through structured education, continuous training, and national/international collaboration.

Our Objectives

  • Train instructors through certified seminars & workshops
  • Promote up-to-date knowledge of driving safety trends
  • Collaborate with government agencies and NGOs
  • Support international cooperation in driver education
  • Maintain a publicly accessible instructor registry
