Please review the membership categories before proceeding with your registration.

1. Affiliate

Candidates of the institute's professional training or students of institutions offering professional instruction programmes.

2. Technician

Individuals with practical/vocational skills, minimum educational qualification (FSLC/JSSC/BECE/WAEC/NECO), and a technical/technician certificate from the institute.

3. Member

Holders of tertiary education (minimum NCE/ND) and a professional diploma in driving instruction from an accredited institution or the institute. This includes degree holders in Transport disciplines.

4. Fellow

Must have been a member for at least 10 years, with proven integrity and financial commitment to the institute.

5. Honorary Fellow

Individuals who have significantly contributed to driver education and road safety in Nigeria. Appointed by the Council of the Institute based on integrity and commitment.

Note: Monthly dues vary depending on the level you register for.
